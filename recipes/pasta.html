Mouth dripping Pasta

Ingredients

  • 2 cups "00" or all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Steps to make a good pasta

  1. The Well: Mound flour on a clean surface and make a "well" in the center. Crack eggs into it and add oil and salt
  2. Mix: Gently whisk eggs with a fork, gradually pulling flour from the "walls" until a shaggy dough forms
  3. Knead: Knead vigorously for 8–10 minutes until the dough is smooth and elastic
  4. Rest: Wrap in plastic and let rest at room temperature for 30 minutes to relax the gluten
  5. Roll & Cut: Roll out into thin sheets (translucent enough to see through) and cut into your desired shape, like tagliatelle or fettuccine
  6. Cook: Fresh pasta cooks fast—usually just 2 to 4 minutes in boiling salted water
